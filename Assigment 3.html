Recipies Of The Day: Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonera is a classic itallian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta and pepper. it's simple, delicious, and perfect for any occasion

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Panacetta
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps to Prepare

  1. Boil the spaghetti in sailted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with grated cheeses.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix with panacetta.
  5. Remove from the heat and stir in the egg and cheese mixture.
  6. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.
